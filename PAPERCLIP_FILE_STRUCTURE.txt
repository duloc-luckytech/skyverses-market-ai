STATE MANAGEMENT
================

Page Level (PaperclipAIAgents.tsx):
- isStudioOpen: boolean

Workspace Level (PaperclipAIAgentsWorkspace.tsx):
- activeTab: 'canvas' | 'runs' | 'settings'
- selectedDept: 'ceo' | 'marketing' | 'devops' | 'sales' | 'hr'
- taskInput: string
- isRunning: boolean
- taskResults: TaskResult[]
- canvasNodes: CanvasNodeState[]
- threadData: ChatMessage[] (per department)
- skills: selected skills per department
- selectedModel: LLM model choice
- activityLog: ActivityLog[]


LOCAL STORAGE KEYS
==================

Workspace Vault:
- skyverses_PAPERCLIP-AI-AGENTS_vault (prefix)
  └── _thread_{deptId} — Chat history per department
  └── _brief_{deptId} — Task brief per department
  └── _skills_{deptId} — Selected skills per department

Max thread turns: 10 (20 messages total)
