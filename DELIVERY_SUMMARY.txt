🎯 FEATURE OVERVIEW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Users can now upload Word documents (.docx) to the AI Slide Creator.
The system automatically:

  1. Extracts slide titles from document headings (H1-H6)
  2. Collects body content as formatted bullet points
  3. Auto-populates slide count (4-12, based on outline length)
  4. Auto-populates deck topic (comma-separated heading titles)
  5. Seamlessly integrates with existing AI deck generation
  6. Generates complete presentations with backgrounds
